Services

From individual leaders to entire systems — engagements designed to meet you where you are and take you where you need to go.

For Leaders

Individual & Executive Coaching

Personal mastery coaching for leaders facing high-stakes challenges, transitions, or the need to elevate their presence and influence.

Executive Coaching

One-on-one coaching that develops a leader's capacity for grounded presence, intuitive decision-making, and harmonious influence. We use somatic practices to help you see yourself and your environment in new ways.

  • Monthly coaching sessions with daily practice commitments
  • Centering, grounding, and energy mastery practices
  • Reactivity pattern diagnosis and transformation
  • Strategic application to live business challenges

Personal Innovation Retreats

Intensive individual retreats for leaders at inflection points. Step out of the whirlwind to inquire into your own heart, discover the source of your power, and design your next chapter from a place of deep presence.

  • Immersive, nature-based inquiry
  • Mastery assessment and practice design
  • Declaration of development vision and goals
  • Enrollment of support partners and accountability
For Teams

Team & Group Development

Helping senior teams develop the trust, dialogue skills, and collective mastery needed to lead complex change together.

Senior Team Synergy

Team-building engagements that develop embodied leadership presence and empathetic, caring relational dynamics within executive teams. Includes Aikido-based partner exercises, dialogue facilitation, and team chartering.

  • Leadership team protocols and decision-making processes
  • Communication links and conflict resolution processes
  • Mutual development covenants and coaching circles
  • 360-degree feedback and team alignment

Leadership Circle

A structured peer development process for leadership groups. Participants practice dialogue, give and receive coaching, and hold each other accountable for personal mastery commitments.

  • Coaching/requests circle format
  • Leadership presence assessment and feedback
  • Cultural principle modeling and behavioral definition
  • Team development goals and measures
For Organizations

Systems Transformation

Whole-system change engagements for organizations facing complex, accelerating challenges that require breakthrough results.

Systems Integration

Complex transformation engagements that address culture, strategy, and structure simultaneously. We facilitate the design cycle — from connecting and sensing through incubating, designing, and declaring breakthrough initiatives.

Stakeholder Fulfillment

Large-group engagement processes that build connectivity across complex stakeholder ecosystems. Open-circle networking, influence planning, and whole-system dialogue cascades that generate coherent momentum.

Business Assessment

Comprehensive organizational assessment using our culture survey and mastery assessment instruments. Diagnose the current state, identify leverage points, and design targeted interventions for breakthrough.

Experiential Programs

Seminars & Training

Group programs that provide direct, embodied experience of the principles and practices. Scalable from 10 to 100+ participants.

The Practice Lab Seminar

A one-to-several-day seminar in which participants directly experience the principles, practices, and applications of embodied presence and decisive collaboration. Conducted with hundreds of leaders in over fifty organizations worldwide.

Format: 10–100+ participants · 1–3 days · casual clothing, room for movement

Seminar Structure

  • Energy Lab — Experience embodied presence: centering, grounding, flowing, extending
  • Collaboration Lab — Practice blending, listening, and co-creation with partners
  • Innovation Lab — The Crucible: multi-force simulations under pressure
  • Application Lab — Design real-world mastery practices and commitments

Mastery Training

Ongoing training programs that develop internal facilitators and change agents. Participants learn to facilitate the somatic exercises, dialogue processes, and design cycle within their own organizations.

Facilitator Development

  • Somatic facilitation toolkit and exercise sequences
  • Dialogue facilitation across four levels of attention
  • Design cycle facilitation for breakthrough initiatives
  • Context setting: who / what & why / how
  • Debrief dialogue: what happened / so what / what now
Diagnostic Tools

Assessment Instruments

Research-based diagnostic tools for measuring individual and organizational readiness for transformation.

The Mastery Assessment

A seven-dimension self-assessment measuring energy mastery capabilities across the full spectrum of leadership presence — from grounded balance to inspired not-knowing. Used to diagnose development needs and track progress over time.

Take the Assessment

The Culture Survey

An organizational culture assessment that measures the coherence of leadership practices, collaborative norms, and the conditions for breakthrough. Identifies systemic leverage points and cultural patterns that support or inhibit transformation.
